The Adara Scholarship Foundation, a Norwegian-based organization, invites applications from qualified Ugandan students pursuing higher education.

Our Mission

The Adara Scholarship Foundation, is dedicated to extending fully funded education opportunities to poverty-disadvantaged students who have been admitted in the public universities in Uganda, building a brighter future for humanity.

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Excellence

Minimum CGPA of 3.0 (all courses)

Documentation

Valid academic documents and enrollment proof

Financial Need

Demonstrated financial disadvantage

Citizenship

Ugandan citizenship required

Application Guidelines

To ensure transparency and fairness, all applications must be submitted electronically. Physical applications will not be accepted. This process helps us avoid activities such as nepotism, cronyism, and favoritism during the selection period.

Important Dates

Application Period:
October 9th - December 10th, 2024

Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee of UGX 50,000 is required to demonstrate commitment to the scholarship. However, successful applicants will receive a full refund upon confirmation.

Required Documents

Academic results, admission letter, and relevant certificates are required. Falsification will lead to application cancellation.
